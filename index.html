The problem with addiction is how society treats addicts

Whilst attempting to reduce and prevent addiction, the perfect system for keeping people addicted was discovered. This system demonises and dehumanises addicts, segregates them from our communities and forces them into poverty, disease and crime.

This system makes reintroduction into society almost impossible.

Introducing Project Phoenix


Project Phoenix is a community owned and directed program to get addicts integrated back into working society.

We distribute the cost, risk and ownership of the reintogration of addicts into society. The program is created by experts and professionals. The funding is covered by the creation and issuing of a security token. This token also incentivises contribution in the creation of the program by being used as a reward.

The plan is that if we create the correct system, then addicts can reconnect with society, find a purpose and a life worth living, then contribute again. Reintegrated individuals will then repay back into the project - like a student loan. The combination of publicly owned security tokens that are linked to the reintegration of an addict into society essentially allows us to incentivise the general public into action by creating financial reward for their support.

Created by experts

All aspects of the recovery, education, training and resettlement program are created by experts. They are medical doctors, psychologists, lawyers and more.



Funded and owned by the public

Anyone can support Project Phoenix by buying FIRE tokens (more on these bellow). These pay for facilities, staff and expenses. But buying FIRE tokens is not like giving away to charity.



Investment not charity

FIRE tokens help in the distribution of profits and other related financial benefits among investors. After successful completion of our program, individuals will pay back Project Phoenix - just like a student loan. This will increase the value of FIRE tokens. They also make supporters co-owners of Project Phoenix.
