The recovery program.

Project Phoenix is community driven. This means that our program is created by its supporters. They are medical doctors, psychologists, lawyers and everyday people from around the world. We will create the program by facilitating collaboration among them. However, collaboration alone is not enough. Debate quickly becomes messy and unproductive. We solve this by incorporating a voting mechanism called Issue-Based Direct Democracy.


IBDD

Designed by Max Kaye, in this system everyone is on an even footing because the source of new knowledge is unpredictable. It also makes individuals specialise around their fields of knowledge, to get the most out of them. It weighs conjecture and criticism without the need for authority and can scale to any user base. It does all this by using markets. The core idea of IBDD is to use a market to trade votes on different issues.


Market Setup

IBDD contains two tokens. These tokens are not exchangeable for normal currency. The first is a vote token. The second is a Capital token. These are tradeable with each other - the rates depending on supply and demand. Capital is used to suggest new policy and upon the proposal of a new policy each user receives one vote token. By default if the vote is not used, it goes up for auction. Capital is constantly redistributed basic income style, to keep the system egalitarian and to incentivise acting earlier rather than later. By using IBDD we ensure that good policies percist and bad policies get challenged. It also makes suggesting bad policy a waste of resources since you need to spend capital to defend it.

How we fund Phoenix

We will fund project phoenix by the creation and offering of a security token. FIRE tokens represent ownership and aid in the distribution of profits and other financial benefits among holders.

Participants of Project Phoenix will pay back - like a student loan. This will increase the value of FIRE tokens. This means that we have created a new economic incentive structure around the rehabilitation of individuals.

FIRE tokens also allow us to reward community contribution by issuing rewards for helping formulate the recovery program.
